\section{Weeding out easy lists}

Let $H \DefinedAs (X, E)$ be a finite $k$-uniform hypergraph.  Let $P \DefinedAs \parens{P_1, \ldots, P_n}$ be an ordered partition of $X$ and $\vec{t} \in \IN_{\geq 2}^n$.  An edge $e \in E$ let $\A_e \DefinedAs \setb{i}{\irange{n}}{\card{e \cap P_i} \geq t_i}$ be the \emph{compatible indices} for $e$.  Then $P$ is $\vec{t}$-acceptable if $\sum_{i \in \A_e} t_i \geq k$ for each $e \in E$.

\begin{question}
Which hypergraphs have $\vec{t}$-acceptable partitions for some $\vec{t} \in \IN_{\geq 2}^n$?
\end{question}
