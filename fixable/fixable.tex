\title{fixable}
\maketitle

Let $G$ be a loopless multigraph and $L$ a list assignment on $V(G)$.  For different colors $a,b \in Pot(L)$, let $S_{a,b}$ be all the vertices of $G$ that have exactly one of $a$ or $b$ in their list; more precisely, $S_{a,b} = \setb{v}{V(G)}{\card{\set{a,b} \cap L(v)} = 1}$.  We say that $G$ is \emph{$L$-fixable} if
\begin{enumerate}
\item $G$ has an edge coloring $\pi$ such that $\pi(x) \in L(x) \cap L(y)$ for all $xy \in E(G)$; or
\item There are different $a,b \in Pot(L)$ such that for every partition $P_1, ..., P_k$ of $S_{a,b}$ into sets of size at most two, 
      there is $J \subseteq \irange{k}$ so that $G$ is $L'$-fixable where $L'$ is formed from $L$ by swapping $a$ and $b$ in $L(v)$ for every $v \in \bigcup_{i \in J} P_i$.
\end{enumerate}
