For a graph $G$, let $\C_G$ be the $\chi(G)$-colorings of $G$.
For a graph $G$ and $S \subseteq V(G)$, the \emph{stolidity} of $S$ is
\[\stol_G(S) \DefinedAs \frac{1}{\card{\C_G}}\sum_{\pi \in \C_G} \frac{\card{\pi(S)}}{\card{S}}.\]


Of particular interest are graphs $G$ where $\stol_{G-v}(N(v))$ is near $1$ for all $v \in V(G)$.
